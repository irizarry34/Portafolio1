Christian Irizarry

Medical Technologist

I am a Clinical Laboratory Application Specialist with a strong background in the development and integration of Laboratory Information Systems (LIS) and hands-on experience managing analytical laboratory equipment. Recently, I have strengthened my technical expertise with specialized training in pathology informatics and software engineering, focusing on leveraging information technologies to improve clinical efficiency. I completed a Pathology Informatics course with the ASCP, gaining critical competencies in laboratory data management, informatics workflows, and regulatory compliance, emphasizing the enhancement of clinical decision-making through effective information use. My objective is to apply this knowledge to optimize clinical application management, streamline laboratory processes, and support medical and laboratory teams with technological solutions that increase diagnostic accuracy and operational efficiency.
